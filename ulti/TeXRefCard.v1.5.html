Delimiters

Left and right delimiters will be enlarged if they are prexed
with \left or \right. Each \left must have a matching
\right, one of which may be an empty delimiter (\left. or
\right.). To specify a particular size, use the following:
\bigl, \bigr \Bigl, \Bigr \biggl, \biggr
You can also say \bigm for a large delimiter in the middle of a
formula, or just \big for one that acts as an ordinary symbol.
